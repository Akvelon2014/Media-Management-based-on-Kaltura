execute()method
public override function execute(notification:INotification):void

The command handles the MEDIA_READY notification, according to the type of entry (live or recorded), the value of the autoPlay flashvar (whether the entry should play immediately on being loaded or after the user presses play. In the case of a live entry, the player cannot begin to play imemdiately but goes through a process which determines whether the live stream is currently on-air or offline.
