minimumWidthproperty
minimumWidth:Number  [read-only]

Gets the minimum width for the MediaControlsView (read-only). The minimum width of the MediaControlsView is determined by the sum of muteToggleWidth, playPauseWidth, volumeControlWidth minimumInfoViewWidth and total horizontal padding.
