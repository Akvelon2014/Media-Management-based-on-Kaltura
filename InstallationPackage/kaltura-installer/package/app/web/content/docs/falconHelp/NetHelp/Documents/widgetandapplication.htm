Widget and Application Studio

Kaltura’s application studio enables you to configure your player instances. The application studio is accessed through the KMC Studio tab. \

•      Player designs - design your player skin - select the size, color, fonts, etc.

•      Player branding - include your logo on the player as a watermark.

•      Player functionality and added features - decide what buttons to display on the player, create your own custom buttons, and decide what features to enable as part of playback (for example, subtitles, sharing, etc.)

•      Player templates - select pre-defined player templates, or create your own.
