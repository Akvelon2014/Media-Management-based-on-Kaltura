Related Videos Gallery

The Related Videos feature can be used to display and share related videos to your viewers. Related videos are displayed according to your selection, either when a video is completed. When you select a related video, the viewer is either redirected to the specified URL, or the related video is loaded in the player.

  To configure Related Videos

1.   Select the source.

2.   Configure Autoplay for Related Videos.
