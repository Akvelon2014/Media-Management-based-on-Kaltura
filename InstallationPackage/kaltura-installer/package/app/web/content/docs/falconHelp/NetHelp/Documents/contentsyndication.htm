Content Syndication

Content syndication is a way of making your content available to multiple other sources. This creates a form of leverage that gives you the ability to create your content once while maximizing your exposure on the Internet by distributing your content in many places at the same time. Content syndication is a win-win for both the publisher and the webmaster. As a result of this type of distribution, publishers receive increasingly more exposure for their content and webmasters receive new source for additional content.
